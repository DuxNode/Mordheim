**Source:** Nemesis Crown Supplement · **Setting:** Nemesis Crown

The province of Nuln is respected across the Empire as the home to the finest handguns and war machines, save for those constructed by the Dwarfs. Graduates from the Imperial Gunnery School are highly prized for their skills in training artillery on vulnerable targets with unerring accuracy and maintaining war machines.

Perhaps the most surprising fact is that the Imperial Gunnery School does not create new weapons of war. Such tasks are executed by the College of Engineering, either the well known one in Altdorf or the Gunnery School's neighbouring college in Nuln. Once the Colleges of Engineering create a war machine, the Imperial Gunnery School trains gunners to use them.

This creates a high demand for their services with only a limited number available at any one time. The school refuses to lower its standards, concentrating on producing better skilled gunners and engineers rather than a larger number of sub-standard graduates.

Most students at the school come from the Imperial nobility — sons who show talents in mathematics or an interest in things that go boom, and who are not immediately in line for succession. One of the requirements of the school is that all students undergo fieldwork to see how weapons perform in the field. They are sent out under the tutelage of a Senior Gunnery Officer and an Instructor, who evaluates the aptitude of the students and whether they are likely to graduate.

## Warband Special Rules

**Impeccable Care:** Students are taught to take proper care of their equipment and to repair weapons should they become damaged. They can always use the reduced cost for black powder weapons listed in their starting Equipment List, and gain an additional +2 on rare rolls to find any black powder weapons.

**Properly Used:** The students know the proper way to use their weapons. When using the optional Misfire rules, if a one is rolled, roll a second dice. On a 3+ the misfire is ignored (the shot still misses, but the weapon does NOT explode).

**Proud To A Fault!:** Members of this warband NEVER use any non-black powder ranged weapons. This prohibition does NOT apply to hired swords or dramatis personae, as they are hired help and have not received proper Nuln training.

## Choice of Warriors

A Gunnery School warband must include a minimum of three models. You have 500 gold crowns to recruit your initial warband. The maximum number of warriors in the warband may never exceed 15.

- **Senior Gunnery Officer:** Each warband must start with a single Senior Gunnery Officer, no more, no less.
- **Instructor:** A warband may include up to one Instructor.
- **Senior Student:** A warband may include up to one Senior Student.
- **Underclassmen:** A warband may include up to two Underclassmen.
- **Sons of the Guns:** A warband may include any number of Sons of the Guns.
- **Marksmen:** A warband may include up to 7 Marksmen.
- **Pistoliers:** A warband may include up to 5 Pistoliers.

## Special Equipment

The following items are available to this warband from the Trading Post.

### Double-barrelled Duelling Pistol
**Cost:** 45 + 2D6 gc (80 + 4D6 gc for a brace) · **Availability:** Rare 11 (Rare 12 for a brace)

A natural evolution from the double-barrelled pistol, but it has proven less popular than envisaged. **Range:** 9" · **Strength:** 4

**Accuracy:** All shots and close combat attacks from a duelling pistol have a +1 bonus to hit. **Prepare Shot:** A complete turn to reload; may only fire every other turn. With a brace, may fire every turn. **Save Modifier:** A warrior wounded must make his armour save with a –2 modifier. **Hand-to-hand:** May be used in close combat; grants +1 Attack in the first round resolved at S4 with –2 save modifier. **Double-barrelled:** May fire one or both barrels. When firing both, roll one dice to hit, resolve each wound separately. After firing both barrels place 2 tokens; remove one per turn.

### Double-barrelled Handgun
**Cost:** 60 + 2D6 gc · **Availability:** Rare 10

**Range:** 24" · **Strength:** 4

**Prepare Shot:** A complete turn to reload; fire only every other turn. **Move or Fire:** May not move and fire in the same turn. **Save Modifier:** –2 modifier to armour save. **Double-barrelled:** As above.

### Double-barrelled Pistol
**Cost:** 25 + 1D6 gc (46 + 2D6 gc for a brace) · **Availability:** Rare 9 (Rare 10 for a brace)

**Range:** 6" · **Strength:** 4

**Prepare Shot:** A complete turn to reload; fire every other turn. With a brace, fire every turn. **Save Modifier:** –2 modifier to armour save. **Hand-to-hand:** As duelling pistol above. **Double-barrelled:** As above.

### Hand-held Mortar
**Cost:** 80 + 2D6 gc · **Availability:** Rare 12

**Range:** 24" · **Strength:** 4

**Prepare Shot:** Fire every other turn. **Move or Fire:** May not move and fire. **Save Modifier:** –2 to armour save. **Scatter:** On a miss, the shot lands 2D6" in a random direction. **Experimental:** Always subject to the optional Blackpowder Weapons misfire rules. **Explosive Radius:** The target and any models within 1½" each take a single S4 hit.

### Hersten-Wenkler Pigeon Bombs
**Cost:** 30 + 2D6 gc · **Availability:** Rare 8

**Range:** Unlimited · **Strength:** 4

**Move or Fire:** May not move and fire. **Explosive Radius:** As Hand-held Mortar above. **Temperamental:** Roll a D6 instead of using BS. On 5–6 the bomb hits; on 2–4 the fuse fails and the pigeon explodes harmlessly in the air; on 1, the pigeon explodes in the hero's hands — he and everyone within 1½" takes a S4 hit. **Pigeon Roost:** Once a Hero buys pigeon bombs, his supply is replenished at the start of each new game.

### Repeater Handgun
**Cost:** 60 + 2D6 gc · **Availability:** Rare 11

**Range:** 24" · **Strength:** 4

**Save Modifier:** –2 to armour save. **Move or Fire:** May not move and fire. **Fire Thrice:** May fire up to three shots; if more than one shot is made the to-hit roll is at –1. **Experimental:** Always subject to the optional Blackpowder Weapons misfire rules. **Slow Reload:** To reload the warrior must do nothing for a complete turn.

### Repeater Pistol
**Cost:** 30 + 2D6 gc · **Availability:** Rare 9

**Range:** 6" · **Strength:** 4

**Save Modifier:** –2 to armour save. **Fire Thrice:** As Repeater Handgun above. **Experimental:** Always subject to optional Blackpowder Weapons misfire rules. **Quick Reload:** The pistol will always be able to fire at least one shot. After shooting more than one shot, the model must spend a complete shooting phase without shooting and without being in combat before firing multiple shots again. **Not a Club:** May be used as a normal pistol in the first round of combat only. Cannot count as an additional hand weapon after that.
