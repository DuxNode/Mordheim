<div class="warband-status grade-1b">Grade 1b — Fanatic Online #19 (Mercenary variant)</div>

**Source:** Fanatic Online #19 · **Setting:** Mordheim

Ostermarkers are used to harsh odds and battling alone to defend their farms against mercenaries and other brigands. Their years of isolation and self-reliance have made them hardy warriors indeed, and, as such, they do not give up easily or balk in the face of outnumbering foes.

Ostermarkers follow all the rules for Mercenary warbands with the additions below. Unit profiles, equipment lists, and warband composition are identical to the Mercenaries (see [Mercenaries](/human/mercenaries-reikland)).

---

## Special Rules

**Stubborn:** Ostermarkers (Heroes and Henchmen) may re-roll a failed Leadership test for being all alone and for routing. A warrior or warband must accept the second roll, however, regardless of the result.

**Guard Hound:** Any captain of an Ostermarker warband may have a wardog as part of his starting equipment, to represent the beast who once protected his farm and holdings from bandits and other, fouler creatures.

---

## Choice of Warriors

A Mercenary warband must include a minimum of three models. You have 500 gold crowns available to spend. The maximum number of warriors in the warband may never exceed 15.

- **Captain:** Each warband must have one Captain: no more, no less.
- **Champions:** Your warband may include up to two Champions.
- **Youngbloods:** Your warband may include up to two Youngbloods.
- **Warriors:** Your warband may include any number of Warriors.
- **Marksmen:** Your warband may include no more than seven Marksmen.
- **Swordsmen:** Your warband may include no more than five Swordsmen.

**Weapons/Armour:** A Captain may be equipped with weapons and armour chosen from the Mercenary Equipment list.

**Leader:** Any warrior within 6" of the Captain may use his Leadership characteristic when taking Leadership tests.
