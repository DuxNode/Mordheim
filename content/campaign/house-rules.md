> [!info] Campaign-specific rules
> These are the house rules agreed upon for the **Mordheim Australis 2027** campaign. They apply to all games played in the campaign unless otherwise noted. Discuss any questions with the campaign organiser before your first game.

---

## Critical Hits

A roll of a 6 to wound no longer causes a critical hit automatically. Instead, if you have wounded the target, roll another D6. On a result of a 6, a critical hit is caused — use the advanced critical tables from the main rulebook.

> **Special case — Art of Silent Death:** Skaven heroes with the Art of Silent Death skill cause critical hits on a 5 or 6 on this second roll, as normal for that skill.

---

## Magic Miscasts

Magic is dangerous. Rolling a **double 1** when casting a spell results in a **miscast** — roll on the miscast table (to be provided by the campaign organiser).

- You may **never** re-roll a miscast result using any skill or item.
- You **may** use a Lucky Rabbit's Foot to re-roll a single die when rolling on the miscast table.

---

## Lucky Items

The following items are limited to **one per warband** (across all warriors combined):

- Lucky Rabbit's Foot
- Lucky Charm
- Holy Relic / Unholy Relic
