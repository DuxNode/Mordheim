title: Warbands
---

All warbands available for the Mordheim Australis 2027 campaign, organised by grade.

**[[reference/warbands/grade-1a/index|Grade 1A]]** — The canonical warbands. Rules sourced directly from the Mordheim Rulebook and official Games Workshop supplements.

**[[reference/warbands/grade-1b/index|Grade 1B]]** — Approved warbands from the Town Cryer and other official GW publications. Fully supported by Quartermaster.

**[[reference/warbands/grade-1c/index|Grade 1C]]** — Extended warbands from community sources. Rules available but not yet approved for the Australis campaign. *(On pause — not available for campaign play until further notice.)*

**[[reference/warbands/unofficial/index|Unofficial]]** — House-rules warbands specific to the Australis campaign. Not submitted to Quartermaster.
