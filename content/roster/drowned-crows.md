The Drowned Crows are a warband of Norse Marauders of Chaos — seafarers and reavers drawn south by rumour of wyrdstone and the promises whispered by gods whose names they do not speak aloud. The name is older than the campaign: taken from the first ship that brought them to these shores, which went down in a squall three days out of port. The survivors waded ashore carrying what they could. They have not discussed going home since.

## Special Rules

**Hárek the Drowned** — Leader. *Barbed Whip* — Whipcrack: +1 A on the charge or when charged, strikes first, cannot be parried. *Enrage* — Warhounds within 4″ gain +1 A while not in close combat.

**Eyvind Twin-Fang** — *Two Weapons* — both blades at −1 to hit (Australis house rule, until Dual-wield is learned). *Parry*.

**Ormarr the Hewer** — *Strikes Last*. −2 to enemy armour saves (Cutting Edge + double-handed).

**Vékell Crow-Tongue** — Wizard — Chaos Rituals. *Mark of Undivided* unlocks 0–3 Gors.

**Refkell the Forsworn** — Condemned. *Causes Fear*.

**Hárek's Hounds** — Animal — gain no experience.
