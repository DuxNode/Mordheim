*Fanatical warrior-inquisitors of the Order of Templars of Sigmar, dedicated to the eradication of heresy, mutation, dark magic and Chaos. Accompanied by Warrior-Priests, Flagellants and Zealots, they have come to purge Mordheim in fire.*

## Choice of Warriors

Minimum 3 models, maximum 12. Starting warband: 500 gold crowns.

- **Witch Hunter Captain:** 1 (required)
- **Warrior-Priest:** 0–1
- **Witch Hunters:** 0–3
- **Zealots:** any number
- **Flagellants:** 0–5
- **Warhounds:** 0–5
