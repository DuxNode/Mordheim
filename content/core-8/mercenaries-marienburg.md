*Sumptuously equipped warriors from the wealthiest trading city in the Old World. Marienburgers are skilled duelists with mercantile contacts throughout the Empire, starting with more gold than any other warband.*

## Special Rules

**Wealthy Start:** Marienburgers begin campaigns with 600 gold crowns (500 + 100 bonus). In one-off games they receive +20% gold crowns.

**Merchant Contacts:** Marienburg warbands receive a +1 bonus when attempting to find rare items in the Trading Post.

## Choice of Warriors

Minimum 3 models, maximum 15. Starting warband: 600 gold crowns.

- **Mercenary Captain:** 1 (required)
- **Champions:** 0–2
- **Youngbloods:** 0–2
- **Warriors:** any number
- **Marksmen:** 0–7
- **Swordsmen:** 0–5
