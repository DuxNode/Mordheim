*Deranged warriors smeared with blood and dirt, following the Shadowlord of the City of the Damned. Men whose hunger for power led them to pledge their souls to dark gods, joined by mutants, Beastmen, and creatures of possessed flesh.*

## Choice of Warriors

Minimum 3 models, maximum 15. Starting warband: 500 gold crowns.

- **Magister:** 1 (required)
- **The Possessed:** 0–2
- **Mutants:** 0–2
- **Darksouls:** 0–5
- **Beastmen Gors:** 0–3
- **Brethren:** any number

## Special Rules

### Mutations
Mutations may be bought for Mutants or Possessed only at recruitment. Second and subsequent mutations for the same model cost double.

| Mutation | Cost | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| Daemon Soul | 20 gc | 4+ save vs spells/prayers |
| Great Claw | 50 gc | Extra attack at +1 Strength; no weapon in that arm |
| Cloven Hoofs | 40 gc | +1 Movement |
| Tentacle | 35 gc | Reduces opponent's attacks by 1 in close combat |
| Blackblood | 30 gc | If wounded in CC, models in contact take S3 hit |
| Spines | 35 gc | Models in contact suffer auto S1 hit each CC phase |
| Scorpion Tail | 40 gc | Extra S5 attack; S2 vs poison-immune |
| Extra Arm | 40 gc | Extra attack with single-handed weapon or shield |
| Hideous | 40 gc | Model causes Fear |
