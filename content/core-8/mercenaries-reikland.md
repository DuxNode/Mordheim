<div class="warband-status grade-1a">Core 8 — built into Quartermaster by default</div>

*Disciplined warriors in service to the Grand Prince of Reikland, famed for their martial loyalty and exceptional archery. Reiklanders disdain fashion for well-made practical wargear, and their officers command unusual loyalty from the men.*

## Special Rules

**Extended Leadership:** Fighters may use their Captain's Leadership if within 12" rather than the usual 6".

**Reikland Marksmen:** All Marksmen add +1 to their Ballistic Skill.

## Choice of Warriors

Minimum 3 models, maximum 15. Starting warband: 500 gold crowns.

- **Mercenary Captain:** 1 (required)
- **Champions:** 0–2
- **Youngbloods:** 0–2
- **Warriors:** any number
- **Marksmen:** 0–7
- **Swordsmen:** 0–5
