*Holy warrior-priestesses of the Order of Merciful Sisters of Sigmar, based at their fortress convent on Sigmar's Rock in the River Stir. Charged by Sigmar himself to gather wyrdstone and lock it away where its corruption cannot spread.*

## Choice of Warriors

Minimum 3 models, maximum 15. Starting warband: 500 gold crowns.

- **Sigmarite Matriarch:** 1 (required)
- **Sister Superior:** 0–3
- **Augur:** 0–1
- **Sigmarite Sisters:** any number
- **Novices:** 0–10

## Special Skills

**Sign of Sigmar:** Possessed or Undead opponents lose their first attack against this model in round 1 of combat (minimum 1).

**Protection of Sigmar:** Spells targeting this model are nullified on a 4+.

**Utter Determination** *(Matriarch only):* May re-roll any failed Rout tests.

**Righteous Fury:** Hates all Skaven, Undead, Possessed, Beastmen, Dark Elves and other evil warbands.

**Absolute Faith:** May re-roll Fear tests; does not test when fighting alone vs multiple opponents.
