*Thralls of Count Vlad von Carstein of Sylvania. Vampires lead shambling Undead hosts through the night-dark streets of Mordheim, gathering wyrdstone to fuel a great necromantic spell that may raise an army of the dead across the Empire.*

## Choice of Warriors

Minimum 3 models, maximum 15. Starting warband: 500 gold crowns.

- **Vampire:** 1 (required)
- **Necromancer:** 0–1
- **Dregs:** 0–3
- **Zombies:** any number
- **Ghouls:** any number
- **Dire Wolves:** 0–5
